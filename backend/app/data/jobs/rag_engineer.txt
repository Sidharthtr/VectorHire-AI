Job Title: RAG Engineer
Company: Cognify AI
Location: Remote (Global)

About Cognify AI:
Cognify AI is a Series B startup building next-generation document intelligence products using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Our customers include legal firms, research institutions, and healthcare providers who need precise, grounded AI answers from their private document corpora.

Role Summary:
We are hiring a RAG Engineer to design, build, and optimize our core retrieval pipeline. You will own the full lifecycle from document ingestion to semantic retrieval and LLM-grounded generation.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement RAG pipelines using LangChain and LlamaIndex
- Build document ingestion systems: chunking strategies, metadata extraction, embedding pipelines
- Optimize ChromaDB and Pinecone collections for production-scale retrieval
- Implement hybrid retrieval (dense + sparse/BM25) for improved recall
- Evaluate retrieval quality using metrics (MRR, NDCG, recall@k)
- Build reranking layers using cross-encoder models
- Integrate Gemini, Claude, and OpenAI models through a unified LLM gateway
- Monitor retrieval pipelines in production using LangSmith

Requirements:
- 1-3 years experience in backend engineering or AI/ML engineering
- Strong Python skills
- Deep understanding of RAG architecture and tradeoffs
- Hands-on experience with LangChain or LlamaIndex
- Experience with at least one vector DB: ChromaDB, Pinecone, Weaviate, or Qdrant
- Familiarity with embedding models (sentence-transformers, OpenAI embeddings)
- Understanding of chunking strategies and their impact on retrieval quality
- Experience with LLM APIs (OpenAI, Gemini, Anthropic)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with reranking models (Cohere, cross-encoders)
- Production experience with vector databases at scale
- LangSmith or other LLM observability tools
- Knowledge of RLHF, fine-tuning workflows

Skills: Python, LangChain, LlamaIndex, RAG, ChromaDB, Pinecone, Weaviate, sentence-transformers, embeddings, Gemini, OpenAI, LangSmith, FastAPI, vector search, reranking
